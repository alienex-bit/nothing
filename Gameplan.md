# Project: "Nothing" - Evolution Roadmap

## 1. Intro Sequence
* **The Big Bang:** Cinematic start sequence transition into primitive Earth.

## 2. Core Features & "Cool Factor"
* **Evolutionary Divergence:** Choose traits at milestones: Speed, Size, or Resilience.
* **Dynamic Environments:** Biomes shift from Volcanic to Oceans to Lush as you evolve.
* **Rare Anomalies:** Glowing particles that grant massive evolution boosts.
* **Adaptive Soundscape:** Minimalist ambient hum evolves into a full orchestral score.
* **Legacy Unlocks:** View your unique evolutionary path in the "Ancient History" gallery.

## 3. Evolutionary Stages
1. **Protocell:** Simple dot, absorbing basic elements.
2. **Microbe:** Cilia and appendages, introduction of movement variety.
3. **Simple Life:** Basic organism structure with biological markers.
4. **Complex Life:** Multi-cellular entity with complex geometry.
5. **Human:** Final stage reached through accumulated choices and survival.

## 4. Technical Goals
* **Input:** Touch/Drag navigation.
* **Loop:** Collision detection with elements -> Consumption -> Growth -> State Check.
